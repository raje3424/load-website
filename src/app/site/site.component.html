FAQ

Why Digital Media ?

We can get the accurate reaction on social media and we can also get very quick feedback.

Can we use Digital Marketing only ?

Digital Marketing is the next big platform where you should concentrate on but, one should not concentrate all efforts only on one medium. One should always have distributed strategy.

What is a successful campaign ?

There are different parameters to measure success of a campaign. Generally we concentrate on views. More views means better the campaign.

What is your method of running a campaign ?

We have a team of full time Designer and a Campaign Manager who are responsible for deciding strategy and execution. We plan and design the posts as per the requirement of client.

How many posts can you make in One day ?

We can make 2-3 specialized posts in a single day but, we advice against posting more than 1 post a day.

How fast can we grow our user ?

User growth is not in our hand and we can’t give you a specific no. We can surely try to attract as many user as possible in short amount of time. But, we can not give a no. Or time period about user growth.

What are the factors of user growth ?

Main factors to attract more users to your page or social media page is to create and post quality content which is either very engaging or which provides some value to users day to day life.

Can we advertise for 1 month and then stop ?

Yes, you can definitely do that. But, Social Media is a long term investment. User engagement and reach improves with time. So we generally advice for long term plans.
